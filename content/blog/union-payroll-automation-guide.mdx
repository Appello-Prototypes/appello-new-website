# Union Payroll Automation for ICI Contractors: Complete Guide

For ICI contractors with unionized workforces, payroll is complex. Workers switch classifications mid-shift, shift differentials apply for nights and weekends, travel pay varies by location, and prevailing wage rules differ by project. Manual union payroll calculations take 12-15 hours per pay period and one error can trigger union grievances costing thousands. Union payroll automation eliminates this complexity.

## The Union Payroll Challenge

### Why Union Payroll is Complex

**Multi-Classification Tracking:**
- Workers switch roles during shift (apprentice → journeyman)
- Different rates for different classifications
- Must track hours by classification
- Complex calculations required

**Shift Differentials:**
- Night shift premiums (typically +$2-5/hour)
- Weekend premiums (typically +$3-7/hour)
- Holiday premiums (typically 2x rate)
- Overtime calculations on premiums

**Travel Pay:**
- Varies by distance traveled
- Different rules by location
- Per diem calculations
- Travel time vs. work time

**Prevailing Wage:**
- Government projects require prevailing wage
- Rates vary by location and classification
- Certified payroll reporting required
- Complex compliance requirements

### The Cost of Manual Processing

**Time Investment:**
- Office manager: 12-15 hours per pay period
- Calculations: 6-8 hours
- Double-checking: 3-4 hours
- Grievance handling: 2-3 hours
- **Total: 23-30 hours per pay period**

**Labor Cost:**
- 25 hours × $30/hour = $750 per pay period
- **Annual cost: $19,500**

**Error Costs:**
- One payroll error = union grievance
- Back pay with penalties: $5,000-$15,000
- Damaged labor relations
- Legal fees: $2,000-$5,000
- **Total: $7,000-$20,000 per error**

**Stress Cost:**
- Sunday night anxiety
- Fear of mistakes
- Burnout risk
- Single point of failure

## How Union Payroll Automation Works

### Automated Classification Tracking

**Multi-Classification Support:**
- Worker clocks in as apprentice
- Switches to journeyman mid-shift
- System tracks hours by classification
- Applies correct rates automatically
- Calculates total pay accurately

**Example:**
- 4 hours apprentice @ $35/hour = $140
- 4 hours journeyman @ $55/hour = $220
- **Total: $360** (calculated automatically)

### Shift Differential Automation

**Night Shift:**
- Worker clocks in after 6 PM
- System applies night premium automatically
- Base rate + $3/hour premium
- Overtime calculated on total rate

**Weekend Work:**
- Worker clocks in on Saturday
- System applies weekend premium automatically
- Base rate + $5/hour premium
- All hours at premium rate

**Holiday Work:**
- Worker clocks in on holiday
- System applies holiday rate automatically
- Typically 2x base rate
- All hours at holiday rate

### Travel Pay Automation

**Distance-Based Travel:**
- System calculates travel distance
- Applies travel pay rules automatically
- Different rates by location
- Travel time vs. work time tracked separately

**Per Diem Calculations:**
- Location-based per diem rates
- Applied automatically based on job location
- Varies by state/city
- Tax implications handled

**Example:**
- Travel to job 50 miles away
- Travel pay: 1 hour @ $40/hour = $40

- **Total travel compensation: $115**

### Prevailing Wage Automation

**Rate Lookup:**

**Certified Payroll:**
