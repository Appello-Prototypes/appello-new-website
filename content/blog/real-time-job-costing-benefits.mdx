# Real-Time Job Costing: Why ICI Contractors Need It Now

For ICI contractors, waiting until month-end to see if a job is profitable is like driving blindfolded. By the time you discover a project is over budget, weeks of overruns have already occurred—too late to make adjustments. Real-time job costing changes that, providing instant visibility into project profitability and enabling proactive project management.

## What is Real-Time Job Costing?

Real-Time Job Costing provides instant visibility into project costs and profitability as work happens, not weeks later. Every timesheet submitted, material purchased, or equipment used updates job costing immediately, giving you the information you need to make decisions today.

### How It Works

**Cost Collection:**
- Timesheet submitted → Labor cost updated instantly
- Material purchased → Material cost updated instantly
- Equipment used → Equipment cost updated instantly
- Subcontractor paid → Sub cost updated instantly

**Profitability Calculation:**
- Revenue (from contract or progress billing)
- Less: All costs (labor, materials, equipment, subs)
- Less: Overhead allocation
- **Equals: Real-time profitability**

**Visibility:**
- Dashboard shows profitability instantly
- Alerts for cost overruns
- Comparison to budget
- Trend analysis
- Project health indicators
