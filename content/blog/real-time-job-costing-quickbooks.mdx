# Real-Time Job Costing with QuickBooks Online: A Contractor's Guide

For ICI contractors, knowing whether a job is profitable has traditionally meant waiting until month-end—when it's too late to make adjustments. Real-time job costing with QuickBooks Online integration changes that, providing instant visibility into project profitability and enabling proactive project management.

## The Job Costing Challenge

### Traditional Job Costing Limitations

**Month-End Reporting:**
- Costs accumulated throughout the month
- Reports generated at month-end
- Profitability visible only after the fact
- Too late to make adjustments
- Reactive decision-making

**The Problem:**
- Project overruns discovered weeks late
- No ability to course-correct mid-project
- Profitability surprises at month-end
- Inability to identify problem projects early

**Real Cost:**
- Projects going 10-15% over budget
- Lost profitability on 20-30% of projects
- Inability to adjust pricing for future bids
- Cash flow issues from unprofitable work

## What is Real-Time Job Costing?

Real-Time Job Costing provides instant visibility into project costs and profitability as work happens, not weeks later. When integrated with QuickBooks Online, all costs—labor, materials, equipment, subcontractors—flow automatically into job costing, updating profitability in real-time.

### How It Works

**Cost Collection:**
- Timesheets submitted → Labor costs updated instantly
- Materials purchased → Material costs updated instantly
- Equipment used → Equipment costs updated instantly
- Subcontractors paid → Sub costs updated instantly

**Profitability Calculation:**
- Revenue (from contract or progress billing)
- Less: Labor costs
- Less: Material costs
- Less: Equipment costs
- Less: Subcontractor costs
- Less: Overhead allocation
- **Equals: Real-time profitability**

**Visibility:**
- Dashboard shows profitability instantly
- Alerts for cost overruns
- Comparison to budget
- Trend analysis
- Project health indicators
